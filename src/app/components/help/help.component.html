The Board Help & Information

Standard Mouse Controls

Hold down your LMB on a piece to drag it to a square.

Right-clicking a square will place a Green Marker.

Right-clicking while holding Shift or Ctrl will place a Red Marker.

Right-clicking while holding Alt will place a Blue Marker.

Right-clicking while holding Ctrl and Alt will place a Yellow Marker.

Holding down your RMB button while dragging to another square will create a Green Arrow.

Holding down your RMB button and Shift or Ctrl while dragging to another square will create a Red Arrow.

Holding down your RMB button and Alt while dragging to another square will create a Blue Arrow.

Holding down your RMB button and Ctrl and Alt while dragging to another square will create a Yellow Arrow.

 Standard Board Controls

Board Size - Changes the size of the board in Pixels.

Free Mode - Toggles the ability to add, remove and move pieces around the board freely.

Coordinates - select inner, outer or no coordinates. You can also select the size out of the outer coordinates.

Reset - Reset the board to a beginning set up of pieces.

Clear - Clear the board, removing all pieces and giving you a clear board.

Reverse - Turn the board 180 degrees

Undo - Undo your last move

Manage Fens - Opens the fen management dialog.

Move History - Displays the move history.

Square Colours - Select what colours you would like the squares to be.
